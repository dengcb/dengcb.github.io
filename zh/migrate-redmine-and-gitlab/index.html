迁移Redmine

  • 补充开发包 如果在18.04下遇到libjbig0版本过高，可以下载降级安装包
    dpkg -i libjbig0_2.1-3.1build1_amd64.deb
  • 修改thin配置

    ---
    chdir: /var/www/artec/mine
    environment: production
    address: 0.0.0.0
    port: 3000
    timeout: 30
    log: log/thin.log
    pid: tmp/pids/thin.pid
    max_conns: 1024
    max_persistent_conns: 512
    require: []
    wait: 30
    servers: 5
    daemonize: true
